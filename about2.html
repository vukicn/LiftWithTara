How Did I Get Here?

After being a competitive gymnast and dancer in my younger years, I was introduced to weight lifting which inspired me to study Kinesiology. Since then, I have earned NASM (National Academy for Sports Medicine) certifications in Personal Training and Nutritional Coaching.

It is my mission to utilize my credentials and personal experience to transfer my expertise to others so they can achieve, but above all, sustain their goals for the rest of their lives.

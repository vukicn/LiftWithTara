About Me

With a decade-long background as a basketball player, culminating in my participation on a D2 basketball team, I discovered my passion for strength training, deep health, and nutrition. This newfound passion inspired me to pursue a degree in Exercise Science and Psychology. During my college years, I had the privilege of interning at Mercy Sports Performance powered by EXOS. This opportunity allowed me to work with a diverse group of clients, including athletes, individuals undergoing physical therapy rehab, and everyday individuals with various fitness goals. I am also a NASM Certified Personal Trainer, PN L-1 Nutrition Coach, and EXOS Performance Specialist, which equips me with the knowledge and skills to provide top-notch training and guidance.

My mission is to leverage my knowledge and experience to help others achieve their fitness objectives and expand their understanding of health and wellness. I am deeply passionate about guiding and empowering my clients, and I approach every interaction with empathy and dedication. At the core of my philosophy is the belief that our health is the most important aspect of our lives.
